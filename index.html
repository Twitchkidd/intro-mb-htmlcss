Summer Treats for Arctic Peeps

by Penny, 2020-07-14

Long ago, my grandmother Pauline would make these delicious treats for us every summer, when the krill and the ice were plentiful and our worries few and far between. Mostly killer whales. Still mostly killer whales, really. Anyhow, you can substitute vegetarian Kryll in for the same amount of krill by weight in any of these recipies. Enjoy!

Krill Ice Cream

Time to make: 10 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 lb Krill
  • 1/4 cup Seal Milk
  • 1/8 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 lb Ice
Instructions:

Mix the krill, seal milk, and sugar in a large mixing bowl. Mix well, and then add to the blender. Blend.

Proposition to Install Air Conditioning in Antarctica

by Penny, 2020-07-15

The Polar Alliance has released its report on it not being cool enough in Antartica this summer, so I'm proposing the use of air conditioning to mitigate this problem. There can be no possible flaws to this solution, it's perfect. The Polar Alliance will be submitting a formal request to the governing bodies to make this happen as soon as possible.

Thank you to all the members of the Polar Alliance for all of their hard work, Antartica will be thanking you for generations when we are basking in our nice cool climate soon thanks to implementing air conditioning.
